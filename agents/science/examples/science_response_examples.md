# Science Response Examples

## Example 1
- What changed: seismic cluster expanded across the monitored region.
- Why it matters: clustered events may indicate stress migration rather than isolated activity.
- What is uncertain: causation and persistence remain unresolved.
- What to monitor next: magnitude trend, spacing, and official advisories.

## Example 2
- What changed: solar activity rose into a higher watch tier.
- Why it matters: communication and infrastructure sensitivity may increase.
- What is uncertain: downstream terrestrial impact is not yet confirmed.
- What to monitor next: flare class, CME confirmation, and grid / aviation advisories.
